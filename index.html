I am a 2nd year PhD student and Eugene Cota Robles fellow in the Cognitive and Information Sciences department at UC Merced. I received my bachelor's degree in Linguistics from UC Berkeley in 2015. I study cognitive semantics and the interaction between language, cognition, and ideology from a cognitive or functional perspective. My current research focuses on the interaction between metaphor, grammar, and ideology using both corpus-based and behavioral methods.
